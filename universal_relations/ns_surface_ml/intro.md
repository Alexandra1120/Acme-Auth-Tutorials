# Universal Description of the Neutron-Star Surface

This tutorial set introduces machine-learning models for describing the surface of rotating neutron stars and related surface quantities. It includes a global-properties notebook and separate demos for the surface shape, logarithmic derivative at the surface, and effective gravity regressors.

Source repository: [gregoryPapi/Universal-description-of-the-NS-surface-using-ML](https://github.com/gregoryPapi/Universal-description-of-the-NS-surface-using-ML)

Reference DOI: [10.1103/PhysRevD.111.083056](https://doi.org/10.1103/PhysRevD.111.083056)
